Our Streamlined ADU Process

Step 1: Consultation and Design

We start by understanding your vision and the unique characteristics of your lot. Our team collaborates with you to create a custom ADU design that fits your needs and maximizes your investment.

Step 2: Permitting and Approvals

Navigating the permitting process can be complex, but we've got you covered. Our experts manage all the necessary paperwork, ensuring your project meets local regulations and requirements.

Step 3: Construction and Installation

Our ADUs are built offsite in a controlled environment, ensuring high quality and quick turnaround. Once complete, we transport the ADU to your lot and handle the installation with precision and care.

Step 4: Final Inspection and Handover

Before you start generating rental income, we conduct a thorough inspection to ensure everything is perfect. Once approved, we hand over the keys, and your ADU is ready for tenants.
